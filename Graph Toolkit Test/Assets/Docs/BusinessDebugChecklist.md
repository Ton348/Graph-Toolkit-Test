# Business MVP Debug Checklist

## Scenarios
- Fresh rented empty lot
- Fully configured grocery store
- Open but no stock
- Stock present but no cashier
- Cashier present but no merchandiser
- Supplier assigned but no delivery amount
- High markup / zero demand
- Multi-business test (2+ lots)
- Save/load restore test

## Core Flow
1. Rent business
2. Assign business type
3. Install required modules
4. Unlock supplier contact
5. Assign supplier
6. Hire cashier
7. Hire merchandiser
8. Open business
9. Manual delivery
10. Auto delivery
11. Shelving
12. Sales
13. Change markup
14. Close business / reopen
